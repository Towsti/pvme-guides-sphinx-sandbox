perks¶

Best PvM perks for the average player¶

(Credits to <@435481585379180554> and <@425520655920791553>)

Note: This channel states the combos and positions of perks on gear, for explanations of the perks check out <#689236234912137266>

Entry level perks¶

These are very good perks to aim for, generally accepted as satisfactory starting perks.

  • Main Hand

    Precise 6

  • Off-hand

    Equilibrium 4

  • Flanking switch

    Flanking 4

  • Two Hand

    Precise 6 + Equilibrium 4

  • Planted Feet switch

    Aftershock 1 Planted Feet

  • Body

    Biting 3 + Impatient 4

  • Legs

    Crackling 4 + Enhanced Devoted 4

  • Shield

    Turtling 4

  • Statius’ Warhammer

    Aftershock 1

Advanced PvM perks¶

Magic¶

  • Wand of the Praesul - Precise 6 Aftershock 1

  • Imperium Core - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2

  • Imperium Core (Optional) - Equilibrium 4 Ruthless 3

  • / Seismic Singularity or Dyed Imperium Core - Flanking 4 Equilibrium 1

  • Staff of Sliske - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1 (If you specifically do not want ruthless 1, and will not be using Invigorating 4 on armor, you may switch this with Aftershock 4 Invigorating 2 + Precise 6 Equilibrium 2)

  • Inquisitor Staff - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1 (If you specifically do not want ruthless 1, and will not be using Invigorating 4 on armor, you may switch this with Aftershock 4 Invigorating 2 + Precise 6 Equilibrium 2)

  • Planted Feet switch - Aftershock 1 Planted Feet

  • Elite Tectonic Robe Top - Biting 4 X + Relentless 5 Crackling 4

  • Elite Tectonic Robe Bottom - Impatient 4 X + Invigorating 4 X

  • Dyed Elite Tectonic Robe Bottom - Impatient 4 X + Enhanced Devoted 4

  • Merciless Kiteshield - Turtling 4

  • Arcane Spirit Shield - Lucky 4 Absorbative 2

  • Kalphite Rebounder - Equilibrium 4 Ruthless 3

  • Ancient Lantern - Planted Feet

Melee¶

  • Khopesh of Tumeken - Precise 6 Aftershock 1

  • Khopesh of Elidnis - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2

  • Khopesh of Elidnis (Optional) - Equilibrium 4 Ruthless 3

  • / Off-Hand Drygore or Dyed Off-Hand Khopesh - Flanking 4 Equilibrium 1

  • Zaros Godsword - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1

  • Terrasaur Maul - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1

  • Noxious Scythe - Aftershock 4 Precise 2 + Equilibrium 4 Ruthless 3

  • / Custom-fit TMW Platebody/Malevolent Cuirass - Biting 4 X + Relentless 5 Crackling 4

  • / Custom-fit TMW Platelegs/Malevolent Greaves - Impatient 4 X + Enhanced Devoted 4

  • / MW Platelegs/Dyed Malevolent Greaves - Impatient 4 X + Invigorating 4 Mobile

  • Malevolent Kiteshield - Turtling 4

  • Masterwork Spear of Annihilation - Lunging 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Aftershock 1 (Can replace with Lunging 4 + Aftershock 1 if only using as switch)

  • Kalphite Defender - Equilibrium 4 Ruthless 3

Ranged¶

  • Off-hand Blightbound Crossbow - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2

  • Off-hand Blightbound Crossbow (Optional) - Equilibrium 4 Ruthless 3

  • / Off hand Ascension XBow or Off hand dyed Blightbound XBow - Flanking 4 Equilibrium 1

  • Seren Godbow - Precise 6 Aftershock 1 + Equilibrium 4 Ruthless 3 (Ruthless 3 isnt required but can be situationally useful)

  • Eldritch Crossbow - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1

  • Elite Sirenic Hauberk - Biting 4 X + Relentless 5 Crackling 4

  • Vengeful Kiteshield - Turtling 4

  • Kalphite Repriser - Equilibrium 4 Ruthless 3

  • Dark bow - Precise 6 Aftershock 1 + Invigorating 4

  • Decimation - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1

  • Hexhunter Bow - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1

  • Ancient Repriser - Aftershock 1 Planted Feet
