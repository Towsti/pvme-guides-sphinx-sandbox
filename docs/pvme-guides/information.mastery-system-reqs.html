mastery-system-reqs¶

Note: For all times listed with “sub”, on-the-dot kill times are acceptable e.g. “sub 10” accepts submissions at a kill time of exactly 10:00.

Initiate Role Reqs¶

  • <@&645851808874496032> - Sub 2:50 7man OR 7man tag in 7-10man AoD Discord

  • <@&645851931842969611> - Two b2b kills of average time on one path: top: 2:45 (no skip), mid 3:15, bottom 3:10

  • <@&645851947940970518> - Average 2:40 min/kill across 3 b2b kills, solo

  • <@&646837770555490314> - Sub 21 solo full run of all three dungeons

  • <@&645851899219673088> - 9 kills in 15 minutes (36 kph pace)

  • <@&645851860808499201> - Sub 9:00 Duo

  • <@&645851874133671947> - Sub 6:00 500% (no BoB)

  • <@&645851845235048458> - Sub 13 Duo Vorago (no BoB)

All submissions require supporting video of kill to be reviewed by staff. If unable to provide your own, then someone else’s recording will suffice. A full screenshot that includes the in game kill timer will also work depending on the circumstance.
